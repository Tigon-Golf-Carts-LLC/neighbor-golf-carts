## VULNERABILITY DISCLOSURE POLICY

### Responsible Disclosure:
We encourage security researchers and users to report security vulnerabilities responsibly. We are committed to working with the security community to verify, reproduce, and respond to legitimate security issues.

### Reporting Guidelines:
1. **Email First**: Send initial report to security@shoresidegolfcarts.com
2. **Include Details**: Provide clear steps to reproduce the issue
3. **Evidence**: Include screenshots, logs, or proof-of-concept if available
4. **Confidentiality**: Keep vulnerability details confidential until resolved
5. **Good Faith**: Test only against your own accounts or with explicit permission

### What to Report:
✅ **Authentication bypasses** or privilege escalation
✅ **Data exposure** or unauthorized access to customer information  
✅ **Injection vulnerabilities** (SQL, XSS, CSRF, etc.)
✅ **Server-side vulnerabilities** affecting system security
✅ **Privacy violations** or data leakage issues
✅ **Payment processing** security issues
✅ **Session management** vulnerabilities
✅ **API security** issues or authentication flaws

### What NOT to Report:
❌ **Social engineering** attacks against employees
❌ **Physical security** issues at business locations
❌ **Denial of Service** attacks or load testing
❌ **Spam or phishing** not related to our systems
❌ **Issues requiring user interaction** beyond normal usage
❌ **Browser-specific issues** not affecting our application
❌ **Third-party services** we don't control
